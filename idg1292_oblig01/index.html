About me:

I'm a 33 year old man that lives with my whife and two children in Batnfordsøra, a rural village between Molde and Kristiansund, on the north-west coast of Norway. Currently I'm working as a full stack web developer at Tibe Molde, while i at the same time try to complete the one year study in Web Design at NTNU Gjøvik.

Before i started working as a web developer i worked ten years a refrigeration project manager, designer and engineer in the commersial market, delivering cooling systems for supermarkets.

Tibe Molde gave me the opertunity to try out web development, and I completely fell in love with programming and system managment as a career, and the industry as a whole.

By participating in the one-year web development program at NTNU gjøvik i hope to strenghen my skills as a web developer, fill in some knowledge gaps, and formally document my knowledge in the field of web development.
